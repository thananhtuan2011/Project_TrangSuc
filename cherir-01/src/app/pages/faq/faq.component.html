HOW TO CHOOSE THE RIGHT JEWELRY SIZES?
Measure your finger at the end of the day when it is at its largest. Avoid measuring your finger when it is hot or cold as this can affect the size. Remember that the two hands differ slightly in size, so measure the specific finger on which you plan to wear the ring.
HOW TO PRESERVE JEWELRY?
Separate your jewelry by metal type to prevent tarnishing when they interact with one another. Store each piece of jewelry in an individual plastic bag. Keep your jewelry out of direct sunlight. Store your jewelry in a dry place.
HOW TO CHOOSE JEWELRY THAT SUITS MY STYLE?
The type of event or place you are going to can dictate what kind of jewelry you should wear. For example, a formal event might require more elegant pieces, while a casual outing might allow for fun and quirky jewelry
HOW TO CHOOSE JEWELRY AS A GIFT?
Consider the Person You Are Buying For Think about their unique style and preferences. What is their distinctive style? Is it funky, boho, retro, dark, elegant, minimalist, or something else? Choose a piece that matches what they like.
